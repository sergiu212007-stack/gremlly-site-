About GREMLY

Discover the story and spirit of the GRML token

Our Origin

GREMLY was born out of a desire to break the typical cycle of hype tokens. Instead of chasing unrealistic promises, GREMLY embraces the chaos of markets with a calm smile. Our digital gremlin lives on the TON blockchain, flicking the switch when FOMO gets too loud.

Mission  Values

  • Honesty – We don’t promise the moon. We promise transparency and fair play.
  • Community – GREMLY is powered by the people who hold it and believe in the vibe. Your voice shapes our direction.
  • Simplicity – A straightforward supply and tax structure means there’s nothing to hide.

The Story

GREMLY came to life when its creators decided to make a token that thrives on authenticity. Our mascot gremlin – mischievous yet lovable – reflects the unpredictable nature of crypto. GREMLY doesn’t instruct you when to buy or sell. It observes, adapts and enjoys the journey.

We invite everyone to write the next chapters of this story by sharing ideas, memes and experiences in our community channels. GREMLY is a blank canvas waiting for your creativity.
